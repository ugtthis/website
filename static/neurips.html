Abstract

comma.ai and the tinycorp are hosting a NeurIPS event on Wednesday, December 3rd, 2025 at their headquarters in San Diego, CA. The event will start at 6:00 PM and will feature fun challenges involving the latest comma.ai and tiny hardware and software such as the comma four, the comma body v2, tinybox, and much more.

This event is strictly invite only. More information about how to attend will be described in this paper.

Food and drinks will be provided.

1 How to get invited

This event is strictly invite only. To get invited, start by going to this link.

Please follow common CTF etiquette: Do not share flags publicly or with other participants. Do not share submission form links. Do not send more than 3 submissions, etc.

Submission deadline is Sunday, November 30th, 2025 at 11:59 PM AoE.

2 CTF Prizes

In addition to getting invited to the event, there are prizes for the top 3 participants who submit all the flags first:
- 1st: comma four + comma body v2 + merch (or $750)
- 2nd: comma four + merch (or $500)
- 3rd: $250 + merch

3 Developing and researching at the event

In addition to fun games, discussions, and talks, access to state-of-the-art comma.ai/tiny hardware will be provided at the event. Participants who wish to develop or research during the event will be given access to hardware such as the comma four, the comma body v2, tinyboxes, etc. Participants are encouraged to bring their own laptops, and develop simple applications or run experiments using the provided hardware and software.

Participants are, for example, encouraged to use the comma body v2 for indoor navigation tasks or sensor data exploration, solve tinygrad bounties, or openpilot bounties, or participate in the research rump talks.
